OFunnel Alerts

Everyday your network expands. But if you aren't on LinkedIn all day long there is no way to know if your network has expanded in some way that is interesting to you. Until now. With OFunnel Alerts, you can now be notified every time someone in your network makes a connection of interest. It could be into an account that you have been trying to get into—it could be to someone that is in a role that you need to hire—or it could even be your competitor that you are trying to keep track of.

Here are a few of the use cases for OFunnel Alerts users:

  • You are trying to get into Nike as an account and don't have any existing relationships. OFunnel will notify you when someone in your network connects to someone at Nike
  • You are connected to one of your competitors on LinkedIn. OFunnel will notify you every time that person makes a new connection
  • You are connected to one of your clients. Wouldn't it be interesting if they suddenly connected to one of your competitors?
  • You are looking for a database administrator. OFunnel notifies you when anyone in your network connects with someone with that job title or headline
